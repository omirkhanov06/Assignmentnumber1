East Kazakhstan

East Kazakhstan is a region with a unique geographical location. Here you can observe almost all types of landscape typical of Central Asia: from mountain peaks to sandy deserts and from coniferous forests to endless flat steppes. At the same time, the main natural landscapes in the east of the country are still mountains.

Maymir Waterfall

A little-visited waterfall on the Maymyr River in eastern Kazakhstan.

Austrian Road

Length of the Austrian road is only 90 kilometers.

Maral Lake

Small mountain lake in a shallow depression among Alpine meadows

Yazovoe

Yazovoe (Karakol) is a small bright and calm lake.
